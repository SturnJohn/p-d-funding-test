Check your project is eligible?

Up to £1,000,000 can be applied for to fund the acquisition of the sporting asset or amenity and refurbishment costs alongside a sale or transfer. Up to £50,000 additional funding (and no more than 20% of the total capital costs) can be applied for to support funding for project development or post-acquisition revenue. Applicants for sporting assets will need to show additional criteria in their application. Please make sure you have read the sporting assets section of the Eligibility criteria guidance.
