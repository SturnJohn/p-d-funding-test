About this fund

Announced July 2011, the Coast Communities Fund is designed to empower coastal regions.

Groups in England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland can bid for funding.

The outcomes and criteria you will need to meet for this fund are UK wide. Devolved administrations will also have country specific boards working with the Big Lottery Fund to help deliver Coastal Communities funding in their areas.

Find out if you’re eligible for this fund

Examples of the activities this fund would consider include:

  • Skils and training initiatives
  • Social enterprise support
  • Community building projects
  • Transport improvements
  • View the full list of activities here

We expect bids for this fund to be valued at £50K or above.
